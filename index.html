Project Asylum

Project Asylum is a narrative-driven horror puzzler where the building itself becomes your enemy. Sent to inspect an abandoned asylum before demolition, you soon realize the blueprint no longer matches reality—hallways shift, cryptic messages appear, and something lurks in the shadows. Armed only with a UV light, uncover hidden clues, navigate the ever-changing maze, and escape before it traps you forever.
